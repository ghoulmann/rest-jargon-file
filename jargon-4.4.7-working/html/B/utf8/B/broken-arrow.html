broken arrow `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **broken arrow**: n. [IBM] The error code displayed on line 25 of a 3270 terminal (or a PC emulating a 3270) for various kinds of protocol violations and “unexpected” error conditions (including connection to a `*down* <../D/down.html>`__ computer). On a PC, simulated with ‘->/\_’, with the two center characters overstruck.
