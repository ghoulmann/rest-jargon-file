TAN `Prev `__  T  `Next `__ -------------- **TAN**: adj. [Usenet, particularly ``rec.arts.sf.written.robert-jordan``] Abbrev. of ‘tangent’, as in “off on a tangent”, and synonym for `*OT* <../O/OT.html>`__. A number of hacker-humor synonyms are used for TAN in some newsgroups. Instances such as BEIGE, OFF-WHITE, BROWNISH-GRAY, and LIGHT BROWN have been observed. It is generally understood on newsgroups with this convention that any color descriptor is a TAN synonym if (a) used as the first word(s) of the topic of a Usenet post, (b) written in ALL CAPS, and (c) followed immediately by a colon.
