SPACEWAR `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **SPACEWAR**: n. A space-combat simulation game, inspired by E. E. “Doc” Smith's *Lensman* books, in which two spaceships duel around a central sun, shooting torpedoes at each other and jumping through hyperspace. This game was first implemented on the PDP-1 at MIT in 1962. In 1968-69, a descendant of the game motivated Ken Thompson to build, in his spare time on a scavenged PDP-7, the operating system that became `*Unix* <../U/Unix.html>`__.
