RTI `Prev `__  R  `Next `__ -------------- **RTI**: /R·T·I/, interj. The mnemonic for the ‘return from interrupt’ instruction on many computers including the 6502 and 6800. The variant RETI is found among Z80 hackers. Equivalent to “Now, where was I?” or used to end a conversational digression.
